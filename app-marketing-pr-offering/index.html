Marketing &

Tech worth talking about

In addition to the design and development of your app, we also can also provide an optional Marketing & PR Offering designed to help you launch your app; gain initial market exposure; and continue to grow your user base.

As a founder, you don’t care about having an app store optimization strategy, a social media strategy, or a paid advertising strategy—you care about finding the right users for your product.

There is no one size fits all solution for finding users, also known as traction. It will be different for every company and it will change over time. What you need is a process to help you find the right way to grow your company, and a partner that can guide you through that process.

What you need is a process to help you find the right way to grow your company…

Nessira Randolph

Director, Marketing
@ Blue Label Labs

 

At Blue Label Labs, we use a process and methodology to help you find traction as quickly and as inexpensively as possible:

  1. 1. Explore all possible channels for selling your product. Cast a wide net and think outside the box.
  2. 2. Systematically test a few of these channels at a time, using real measurable experiments to see which ones can deliver the right kind of users your product.
  3. 3. When you find a channel that works, focus on it exclusively until it stops working—throw all of your resources into building and optimizing a marketing funnel around that channel.

What’s the goal

The goal of all of this is to drive the cost of acquiring customers down. Anything that isn’t contributing directly to that goal is a waste of your time and money.

Blue Label Labs can help you through every part of this process, from the early brainstorming of possible marketing channels, to setting up effective experiments, to implementing and refining a complete marketing funnel based around what we learn from those experiments.

We have expertise in implementing whatever strategy ends up being the right one for your business, whether it’s app store optimization, paid advertising or something else entirely.

We will never try to sell you on a list of services. We are selling a proven process, and the expertise to implement that process in the most effective way for your company.

There is a right way to grow your business. Let us be your guide.

Nessira Randolph

Director, Marketing

Paid User Acquisition

Strategy and execution of any/all paid user acquisition campaigns. This work is all about establishing KPIs, meticulous tracking and measurement using analytics tools, and small advertising “tests” via a number of different ad networks. Measurement and optimization are paramount here.

Services Provided:

  • – Development of goals/key performance indicators (KPIs)
  • – Recommendation of KPI-choice driven analytics solutions
  • – Analytics platforms depending on KPIs: AppBoy, LeanPlum, Appsflyer and/or BranchMetrics
  • – Create ad network testing strategy and execution roadmap
  • – Ad networks used depending on KPIs: Facebook, Twitter, Google and/or AdColony for early stage apps, Supersonic and Liftoff for more established apps
  • – Development of ad copy and graphics
  • – Execution of ad placement, monitoring and optimization
Costs: $2,000 to $3,000 one-time planning fees + $5,000 minimum monthly ad spend (paid directly to ad network/outlet) + 10% to 20% (of ad spend) management fee + $120 per hour for the creation of creative/graphics/videos
