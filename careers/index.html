Director, Sales

Blue Label Labs is a leading New York-based product agency looking for a talented individual to join the team in the role of a Director, Sales. We are a 64-person team that has designed and developed over 250 products. We have a wide-range of capabilities that has seen us create products that span health and wellness, on-demand marketplaces, mobile gaming, IoT and VR/AR.

As a Director, Sales you will be responsible for finding and nurturing relationships with potential clients–entrepreneurs, enterprises and agencies. This role will be both an outbound (pro-active) and inbound (reactive) sales effort. Outbound sales effort supported by existing and new marketing efforts (performed by other, pre-existing team members), email campaigns and other outbound techniques. Inbound sales will be supported by a pre-existing flow of inbound leads.

The day-to-day responsibilities of a the Sales Director at Blue Label Labs include:

  • Conduct sales related activity.
  • Coordinate with Marketing team to oversee execution of co-developed marketing strategies.
  • Identify and track events worth attending, speaking at, sponsoring or hosting.
  • Determine other channels/methods to grow outbound Enterprise sales pipeline.
  • Will report to the Head of Business Development.

We are looking for a detail-oriented Sales Director that has a passion for sales along with the following:

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Established track record and out-performance in the sale of custom digital products and services working alongside globally distributed teams
  • 5+ years of experience in a similar sales role
  • Ability to work independently and manage your own work streams
  • Experience using project management tools (e.g., Trello); sales tools (e.g., SalesForce); marketing tools (e.g., Mailchimp); web platforms (e.g., WordPress) and social media sites (e.g., LinkedIn)
  • A “rolodex” of potential client targets

The compensation and benefits are as follows:

  • Full-time, W-2 employment position.
  • Compensation will consist of roughly 50% base salary and 50% commission and performance-based bonuses–commensurate with experience.
  • Work from home/remote work opportunities–SoHo NYC office for those who prefer to work in an office environment; candidate must be NYC-based or be willing to relocate.
  • Profit sharing and/or equity potential for top performers.
  • Group health, dental and vision insurance plans available.
  • 401(k) with company match up to 5%.

Program Manager

Blue Label Labs is a leading New York-based product agency looking for a talented individual to join the team in the role of Program Manager. We are a 64-person team that has designed and developed over 250 products. We have a wide-range of capabilities that has seen us create products that span health and wellness, on-demand marketplaces, mobile gaming, IoT and VR/AR.

As a Program Manager (PM) you are responsible for leading a product team of designers and engineers to bring our client’s ideas to life. As a PM you will be tasked with the day-to-day management and direction of client projects shepherding them through all phases of the engagement: from discovery and design through to development and testing and finally culminating in the deployment to one or more app stores.

The day-to-day responsibilities of a PM at Blue Label Labs include:

  • Managing all client communication and providing our clients with timely updates on the progress of their project along with answering any queries that may come up.
  • Working with the client, alongside a UX designer, to create a set of user stories to define the vision of the product in the form of wireframes.
  • Managing the budget for the project and ensuring that any issues are identified early, communicated to the client.
  • Creating clickable prototypes of proposed wireframes and designs using tools such as Invision.
  • Working with a UI designer to create and present design concepts based off wireframes and then helping the client choose the best one for their product.
  • Managing the development process using Agile methodologies to define clear milestones and ensuring that the we deliver high-quality, polished builds to the client on-time.
  • Triaging bugs and incoming design changes and working with the development team on a daily basis to ensure that we build a product that exceeds the client’s expectations.
  • Shepherding the created product out of development and testing through final deployment to the app store including the creation of app store metadata, management of the app store approval process and finally deployment into the app store.

We are looking for detail-oriented PMs that have a passion for client service along with the following:

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Established track record of client services work in digital products working alongside globally distributed teams.
  • 3-5 years experience leading software development projects using Agile methodologies (mobile experience is a plus).
  • Ability to work independently and manage their own work streams and that of their project teams to effectively meet deadlines.
  • Experience using tools such as Trello, Invision, Sketch and Photoshop, GitHub, XCode and Android Studio are a plus.
  • Comfortable working remotely.

UX and/or UI Designer

Blue Label Labs is a leading New York-based product agency looking for a talented individual to join the team in the role of a UX and/or UI Designer. We are a 64-person team that has designed and developed over 250 products. We have a wide-range of capabilities that has seen us create products that span health and wellness, on-demand marketplaces, mobile gaming, IoT and VR/AR.

As a UX/UI Designer you are responsible for creating the user experience and user interface that will bring our client’s ideas to life. As a UX and/or UI Designer you will be tasked with the producing all design resources of client projects: from user stories and wireframes through to final UI and icons, and finally culminating in supporting quality assurance testing and app store visual assets for deployment.

The day-to-day responsibilities of a UX/UI Designer at Blue Label Labs include:

  • Managing client and project team communication and providing our clients and the project team with timely updates on the progress of their deliverables along with answering any queries that may come up.
  • Working with the client, alongside a Program Manager, to create a set of user stories to define the vision of the product in the form of wireframes.
  • Creating clickable prototypes of proposed wireframes and designs using tools such as Invision.
  • Producing final design resources, which shall take the form of a well-organized, layered Photoshop or Sketch file representing the final UI design of the product and shall be used by the engineering team in the development of the product.
  • Designing a logo and/or app icon for the product as necessary.
  • Working together with the client and Program Manager on publishing the product to various app stores by providing creative assets such as screenshots and ad creatives.
  • Supporting development and quality assurance testing as necessary to ensure that the developed product meets the goals and expectations of the original design.

We are looking for creative, product-oriented designers that have a passion for user experience design and client service along with the following:

  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Established track record of client services work in digital products working alongside globally distributed teams.
  • 3-5 years experience in user experience and/or user interface design (mobile experience is a plus)
  • Ability to work independently and manage their own work streams and that of their project teams to effectively meet deadlines.
  • Experience using tools such as Trello, Invision, Sketch and Photoshop.
  • Comfortable working remotely.
