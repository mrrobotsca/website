How to Hire a Mobile App Developer That’s Invested in You

Rae Steinbach
9 months ago

Planning and building an app is not an easy process. Even if you have a great idea, you still have to pull together all of the proper resources if you want your app to be a success. One of the most important factors that often gets overlooked is hiring the right mobile app development agency.

Every business knows that they are going to need to hire an expert if they want to work on launching an app. The problem is that many of these people approach finding mobile app developers with the idea that they only need to look for someone with the right technical background. However, there is more to finding the best app development companies than just hiring someone with the right skills.

When you find a mobile app development company that is truly invested in your vision, they can add value that goes beyond just building the app. For companies that are getting ready to develop a mobile app, here are a few tips that can help you find the best agency that is a good fit for your project.

Should You Hire a Mobile App Development Agency or an Individual?

You have a lot of options when it comes to hiring a specialist in mobile app development. For some companies, hiring an individual will be the right choice. For others, they might need an entire team of mobile app developers.

The main advantage of hiring the individual programmer is that you can usually get your app built for a lower price. On the other hand, hiring an individual means that you are expecting that one person to be a jack-of-all-trades in the complex process that comprises mobile app development.

With a mobile app development agency, you have an entire team working on your project. Thus, each team member specializes in different aspects of building an app. You have people that work on design, UI/UX specialists, web development, analytics, and a project manager.

An individual programmer might be strong at one or two of these different disciplines, but there is a good chance that they are just competent (let’s hope) in the other skills that go into building an app.

Portfolio and References of Mobile App Developers

Regardless of whether you hire an individual or a mobile app development agency, you should look into their past work and ask for some references. Look at the quality of design and evaluate the UI/UX of apps that they have built in the past. See if you can talk to the previous clients of these app development companies and try some of the products that they have developed for other projects.

The Best Mobile App Development Companies Are a Partner

Skills and experience are obviously an important part of finding the right developer, but you want a partner that is interested in more than just building the app. If the mobile app developers are really invested in your idea, they are going to have ideas of their own. They will have insights that could make your mobile app better, and they will have experience that can help them to refine your idea.

Mobile Platform-Specific App Development Support

You also have to consider what would be the best mobile app development platform for you and your services. Deciding where you want to build can help you make an informed decision when choosing a developer.

If you choose to develop the app for Android, then you are going to want to find someone that has experience with mobile app development on Android. If you are focused on iOS, it’s important to find a development partner that has experience with iOS.

If you want to build for multiple platforms, you will want to make sure your partner has the necessary skills and resources to develop across different platforms. If you think you might want to expand into things like Smart TV apps or smart watches, then it might be better to hire a company instead of an individual. App development companies are more likely to have different programmers that specialize in development for a wider range of platforms.

After-Launch Mobile Development Support for Your App

A developer’s job doesn’t end when the app launches. Once the app is live on the app store, you may need to fix bugs or change different features based on user feedback.

When you hire someone to help with your mobile app development, you want to know that they are going to be there to provide support and work on updates. Talk to the developer about their commitment to the project. If they are not interested in continuing after the app is built, they are not invested in your idea.

The final tip is to find someone that you are comfortable working with for mobile app development. Building an app is a collaborative process, so you want to make sure the developer fits in well with you and your company. That’s another reason going with an agency could be better: the best mobile app development companies in NYC have a roster of dedicated team members that will make sure you’re completely satisfied with the end result.
